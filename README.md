# Ethical-Hacking-GRC-Readiness-1t157
Let's talk about Governance, Reliability, and Readiness

	As we are aware, a vulnerability disclosure program (VDP) is designed to help the parent organizations assist and reward good cybersecurity research. General cybersecurity hygiene thus isn’t sufficient without a method to document and remedy issues brought forth. Utilized correctly, VDPs are the best practices introducing structured method of cybersecurity vulnerabilities. I liken it to transforming reactive patching into a compliance accelerating mechanism. For this report, I referred to the Officer of Inspector General’s 2025 audit report as well as the DoD Cyber Crime Center 2022 Annual Report on the Vulnerability Disclosure program. Cyber vulnerabilities are often reported in waves, and the applicability of VDP allows valuable insight to be drawn via crowdsourced reports from either volunteer, defense industrial base companies, or even academic subject matter experts.
